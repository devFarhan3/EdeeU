what is edeeu?

EdeeU, Let’s Learn is a resolution, revolution and revival. EdeeU App is for Africa, starting with East Africa and having headquarters in Kampala, Uganda. EdeeU plans to reach all students, individuals and learners who want to study but do not have ample resources. Digitally, EdeeU will let them study and offer startling set of courses, in order for them to learn, create and conquer the global arena. With EdeeU, we see education for ALL.

EdeeU for Africa

Starting with East Africa, EdeeU will spread across the continent making sure, everyone gets the education. EdeeU plans to collaborate with NGOs, WHO and others in order to reach out to all those who are in need. Education can indeed change the course of this continent and we at EdeeU are just playing our part. Are you?
